Common Questions

Q: The app isn't playing sounds
Make sure your device volume is turned up and not in silent mode. Check that the app has permission to play audio.

Q: Sounds stop when I lock my phone
Ensure Background App Refresh is enabled for Baby Sounds in your device settings.

Q: How do I unlock premium sounds?
Tap the "Unlock Premium" button in the app to access all 25+ sounds.

Q: Can I use the app offline?
Yes! Baby Sounds works completely offline once downloaded.
