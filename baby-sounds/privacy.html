Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 9, 2025

Overview

Baby Sounds is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage within our mobile application.

Data Collection

Baby Sounds itself does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its users. However, our app may include third-party advertising services that may collect certain information.

Advertising

We may use third-party advertising services to display advertisements within our app. These services may collect and process certain data including:

  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Cookie data
  • Device type and settings
  • Operating system version
  • Mobile network information
  • App interaction data
  • Ad performance data

Information We Do Not Collect

Baby Sounds does not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or other media files
  • Microphone or camera access
  • Any data about your baby or family

Your Rights

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

On iOS devices:
1. Open your device's Settings
2. Navigate to Privacy & Security
3. Select Tracking
4. Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

On Android devices:
1. Open Settings
2. Navigate to Google settings
3. Select Ads
4. Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

Third-Party Privacy Policies

For more information about how third-party advertising services collect and process data, please review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Baby Sounds is designed for use by parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is intended to be operated by adults on behalf of infants and young children.

Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is minimal risk to your privacy when using Baby Sounds. All app preferences and settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
