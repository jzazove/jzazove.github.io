Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Slide to Slide ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile puzzle game.

Information We Collect

Slide to Slide may collect the following types of information:

  • Game Progress Data: Level completion, star ratings, coins earned, and power-up usage
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: Game session duration, feature usage, and app performance data
  • Advertising Data: Information collected by third-party ad providers to serve relevant advertisements

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save and sync your game progress
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance gameplay
  • Provide customer support

Third-Party Services

Slide to Slide uses third-party services for advertising and analytics, including:

  • Advertising Networks: To display banner, interstitial, and rewarded video ads
  • Analytics Services: To understand how players interact with the game

These services may collect information about your device and usage patterns according to their own privacy policies.

Data Storage and Security

Game progress is stored locally on your device. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Slide to Slide does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Adjust ad tracking preferences in your device settings
  • Contact us with privacy concerns or data deletion requests

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Support page.
