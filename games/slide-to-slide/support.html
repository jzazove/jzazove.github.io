Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn coins?
You earn coins by completing levels. The better your star rating (based on swipe efficiency), the more coins you earn. You can also watch rewarded ads to get extra coins.
What do the power-ups do?
Bomb: Removes an obstacle from the grid
Undo: Reverses your last move
Skip: Allows you to skip a difficult level
How does the star rating work?
Each level has an optimal swipe count. Complete the level in fewer swipes to earn more stars (1-3 stars). Try to find the most efficient path to maximize your rating!
My progress was lost. Can I recover it?
Progress is saved locally on your device. If you've deleted the app or switched devices, unfortunately the progress cannot be recovered at this time. We recommend keeping the app installed to maintain your progress.
I'm stuck on a level. What should I do?
Try approaching the puzzle from different angles. Sometimes the solution requires thinking backwards from the target position. If you're still stuck, you can use coins to skip the level or watch a rewarded ad to earn power-ups.
Can I disable ads?
Slide to Slide is free-to-play and supported by ads. While there's currently no ad-free version, you can earn all power-ups through gameplay without watching optional rewarded ads.
How many levels are there?
Slide to Slide features over 1000 levels! The first levels are hand-crafted tutorials, followed by progressively challenging procedurally generated puzzles.
