Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2025

Overview

Grid Moji is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage within our mobile game application.

Data Collection

Grid Moji itself does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its users. However, our app does include third-party advertising services that may collect certain information.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within our app. AdMob may collect and process certain data including:

  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Cookie data
  • Device type and settings
  • Operating system version
  • Mobile network information
  • App interaction data
  • Ad performance data

Your Rights

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings. On iOS devices, you can limit ad tracking by: 1. Opening your device's Settings 2. Navigating to Privacy & Security 3. Selecting Tracking 4. Disabling "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

Third-Party Privacy Policies

For more information about how Google AdMob collects and processes data, please review: Google's Privacy Policy

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
